# Sidqly Product Overview (AI Readable)

Sidqly is a modular platform designed for the complex operational needs of Islamic giving.

## Key Modules
1. **Zakat Management:** Intake, case review, fund separation, and disbursement tracking.
2. **Qurbani Management:** Share booking, vendor tasking, slaughter proof, and certificates.
3. **Ramadan Operations:** Ration pack tracking, batch distribution, and volunteer shifts.
4. **Manual Payment Review:** Professional verification of bank transfers and screenshots.
5. **Proof Trust Engine:** Secure collection and dignified sharing of impact evidence.
6. **Vendor Portal:** Fulfillment tasks and field proof upload for service providers.
7. **Volunteer Module:** Attendance, shift management, and impact logging.
8. **Board Reporting:** Real-time financial and operational impact dashboards.

## Core Features
- Automated face blurring for dignity-safe proof.
- 6-digit code and QR verification for field delivery.
- Branded donation receipts and completion certificates.
- Multi-currency support and regional localized views.
- Secure, non-indexed donor impact links.
