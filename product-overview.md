# Sidqly Product Overview

Sidqly is a modular operating system designed for the specific needs of Islamic charitable organizations.

## Core Modules
1. **Zakat Management:** Structured intake, manual eligibility review, and disbursement tracking.
2. **Qurbani Operations:** Order management, animal sourcing, slaughter verification, and meat distribution.
3. **Ramadan Drives:** Large-scale food parcel logistics and recipient verification.
4. **Proof & Trust:** A secure engine for capturing field proof without exposing recipient identities.
5. **QR Verification:** Anti-fraud delivery verification.
6. **Premium Reporting:** Board-ready and donor-ready PDF reports.

## Key Benefits
- Replaces scattered WhatsApp coordination.
- Eliminates manual spreadsheet errors.
- Provides real-time status of all missions.
- Builds donor trust with verifiable impact.
