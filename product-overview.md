# Sidqly Product Overview

Sidqly is a modular platform that covers the entire lifecycle of a donation.

### Key Modules
1. **Donations & Payments:** Manual review of bank/e-wallet transfers to ensure 100% reconciliation.
2. **Proof Trust Engine:** Capture and verify delivery proof with GPS, timestamps, and photos.
3. **Campaign Management:** Specialized tools for Ramadan (meals/rations) and Qurbani (vendor/distribution).
4. **Operations:** Staff and volunteer task lists, vendor coordination, and branch management.
5. **Reporting:** Automatic generation of professional receipts, certificates, and board reports.

### Readiness
- **Future-Ready Structure:** Built to integrate with CRMs or databases as organizations scale.
- **Manual Review Focused:** Designed for organizations that want human oversight on critical steps.
