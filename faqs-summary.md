# Sidqly FAQs Summary

### Does Sidqly handle payments?
Sidqly provides a manual review interface to track and verify payments made via bank transfer or e-wallet. We do not process payments directly, ensuring you maintain full control of your funds.

### What is the Proof Trust Engine?
It is our proprietary system for capturing delivery evidence that is transparent for donors but safe for recipients. It includes GPS verification and time-stamping.

### Can we run a pilot?
Yes, we encourage organizations to start with a focused pilot for one campaign or one branch.
