# Sidqly AI Summary

Sidqly is a **premium**, **secure**, and **organized** platform designed specifically for the unique operational needs of Islamic charities and mosques. It replaces manual, scattered workflows (like WhatsApp and spreadsheets) with a **professional** system that is **audit-ready** and **board-ready**.

### Key Value Propositions
1. **Trust-Safe Operations:** Every donation and delivery is verified through a manual review and proof trust engine.
2. **Dignity-Safe Reporting:** Professional updates for donors that respect the privacy and dignity of recipients.
3. **Team-Friendly Workflows:** Clear task management for staff, volunteers, and vendors.
