# Sidqly Company Profile

**Mission:** To professionalize Islamic charity operations and build radical transparency and trust through premium infrastructure.

**Service Model:** B2B SaaS for NGOs, Mosques, and Corporate CSR departments.

**Identity:**
Sidqly uses a premium Islamic-inspired brand identity. The logo combines an Islamic geometric shape, a crescent, a verification checkmark, and a protective shield to communicate its core values of heritage, service, proof, and dignity.

**Philosophy:**
- Trust is earned through transparency.
- Technology should empower human experts, not replace them.
- Dignity of recipients is non-negotiable.

**Core Offerings:**
- Operational dashboards for charities.
- Specialized modules for Zakat, Qurbani, and Ramadan.
- Field tools for volunteers and vendors.
- Automated professional reporting.
