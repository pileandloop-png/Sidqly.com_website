# Sidqly Use Cases

### 1. Mosque Zakat Management
Mosques use Sidqly to separate Zakat and Sadaqah funds, manage charity request intakes, and provide the local community with transparent reports on fund utilization.

### 2. Ramadan Food Drive
Charities run large-scale ration pack distributions using Sidqly to track vendor inventory, volunteer delivery routes, and provide donors with real-time proof of delivery.

### 3. Qurbani Operations
Sidqly simplifies the complex Qurbani workflow, from animal procurement and vendor tasks to meat distribution tracking and donor reporting.

### 4. Corporate CSR/Zakat
Corporate teams use Sidqly to ensure their Zakat programs are audit-ready and to provide professional, branded reports to their stakeholders.
