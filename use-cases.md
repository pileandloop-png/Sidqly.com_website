# Sidqly Use Cases

## 1. Local Mosque Zakat
A neighborhood mosque uses Sidqly to manage local Zakat applications, allowing the committee to manually review cases and track disbursements with a clear audit trail.

## 2. International Qurbani Provider
A large charity manages thousands of Qurbani orders across multiple vendors, using Sidqly to collect proof of slaughter and verify meat distribution in real-time.

## 3. Ramadan Food Drive
A volunteer-led team coordinates the delivery of 1,000 food packs, using Sidqly's QR verification to ensure every pack reaches the intended family.

## 4. Corporate CSR Department
A company tracks its annual Zakat and Sadaqah spending through Sidqly, generating professional impact reports for its annual board review.
